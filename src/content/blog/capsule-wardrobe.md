The capsule wardrobe movement has taught us that fewer, better pieces serve us longer than an overflowing closet. The same principle applies to leather accessories.

## The Three-Piece System

**One travel bag.** The Weekender handles weekends away, gym trips, and overnight stays. Its generous capacity means you never need a second bag for short trips.

**One daily bag.** The Artisan Tote carries your laptop, notebook, and daily essentials. Unlined and lightweight, it adapts to whatever your day requires.

**One wallet.** The Essential Wallet holds cards and cash without bulk. Slim enough for any pocket, considered enough for any occasion.

## Why Three Is Enough

With these three pieces, you cover 95% of life's carrying needs. The key is choosing pieces that work together visually — similar leather tones, complementary silhouettes, consistent quality.

## The Cost of Ownership

A cheap bag replaced every two years costs more over a decade than one quality piece that lasts twenty. When you calculate cost-per-use, the math is clear: buy once, buy well.

Three pieces. One philosophy. Zero waste.
